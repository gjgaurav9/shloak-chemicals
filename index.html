Our Services

Comprehensive Recycling & Recovery Solutions

Two core service verticals designed to maximize waste recovery, minimize environmental impact, and deliver value to our partners.

Plastic Recycling

Transforming plastic waste into premium recycled granules

Our advanced plastic recycling facility processes a wide range of plastic waste — from industrial scrap to post-consumer materials — converting them into high-quality recycled plastic granules ready for reuse in manufacturing.

Premium Recycled Plastic Granules (HDPE, LDPE, PP)
Consistent quality with rigorous testing standards
Reduces virgin plastic dependency by up to 80%
Environmentally responsible waste diversion

Solvent Distillation

Recovering and purifying industrial solvents for reuse

Our solvent distillation process recovers and purifies used industrial solvents — including thinners, acetone, and toluene — restoring them to near-original quality for cost-effective and eco-friendly reuse.

Up to 95% solvent recovery rate
Significant cost savings over purchasing virgin solvents
Reduced hazardous waste and emissions
Compliant with all environmental safety regulations
Our Sources

Where We Source Our Plastic From

We partner with diverse industries and networks to ensure a consistent, high-volume supply of recyclable plastic materials.

Industrial Manufacturing Units

Collecting production offcuts, rejects, and scrap from large-scale manufacturing facilities across India.

Packaging Companies

Sourcing waste films, wraps, containers, and packaging materials from the packaging industry.

Warehouses & Logistics

Recovering stretch wraps, pallets, and protective plastics from warehousing and logistics operations.

Scrap Dealers & Aggregators

Partnering with scrap networks to collect and consolidate diverse plastic waste streams.

FMCG Companies

Processing post-consumer and production waste from fast-moving consumer goods manufacturers.

Automotive Industry

Recycling bumpers, dashboards, and plastic components from automotive manufacturing and end-of-life vehicles.

Local Collection Networks

Working with community collectors and municipal bodies for grassroots-level waste aggregation.
